    Sender Identity

    You can verify one or more Sender Identities using either of

    1. Single Sender Verification
    2. Domain Authentication

    Serenity currently uses Single Sender for Platform Invitation Emails.This document explains the steps to follow in order to setup Single Sender Account in SendGrid for a given environment.

    Single Sender Verification

    Single Sender Verification is a fast way to verify your Sender Identity when you don’t have access to the DNS settings for your domain.

    Advantages

    • Single Sender Verification is a quick method of verification when starting.
    • You can send from a single email address that you confirm ownership of by clicking a verification link in the email’s inbox.

    Disadvantages

    • Single Sender Verification is recommended for testing only.
    • You can send only from the address you verify rather than any address on an authenticated domain.

    Verification

    To protect your sending reputation and to uphold legitimate sending behavior, we require customers to verify their Sender Identities. Follow the steps below to verify a Single Sender:

    1. Under Settings in the navigation bar, click Sender Authentication.
    2. Proceed with Single Sender Verification by selecting Verify Single Sender under Single Sender Verification.
    3. You will be taken to the Single Sender Verification page. Under Create your First Sender, click Create New Sender to load a form modal.
    4. The modal looks like this
    5. Fill in the form fields and to create a new Single Sender
      • From Name - This is a user-friendly name that is displayed to your recipient when they receive their email.
      • From Email Address - This will display to the user as the email address that sent this email. We will send the verification email to the address you enter in this field. If you have not received your verification email after some time, please refer back to the Sender settings and confirm that the "From" email is a valid address.
      • Reply To - If your user hits reply in their email, the reply will go to this address.
      • Company Address, City, State, Zip Code, Country - Your business address.
      • Nickname - A label for your sender identity to help you identify it more quickly. This label is not visible to your recipients.
    6. Check the inbox of the email address that you entered and click the link in the email to verify the Sender address.
    7. If, for any reason, you need to resend the verification email, click the action menu on the Single Sender Verification page and select Resend Verification.
    8. You will now see a page confirming the verification of your address.
    9. Click Return to Single Sender Verification to add more addresses or make any changes to the address you just verified.
